# Migrate a NFT to a full migration

## First you need to go on https://bridge.mynft.com/.

## Choose Migrate NFT:
![1](./Media/UserHowToFM/UHT_FM_1.png)
## Select the wallet
![2](./Media/UserHowToFM/UHT_FM_2.png)
## Choose the origin network if it is not already selected
![3](./Media/UserHowToFM/UHT_FM_3.png)
## Fill your NFT contract address and token id:
![4](./Media/UserHowToFM/UHT_FM_4.png)
## Confirm the information of the NFT to migrate
![5](./Media/UserHowToFM/UHT_FM_5.png)
## Select destination network
![6](./Media/UserHowToFM/UHT_FM_6.png)
## Review the information of the migration
![7](./Media/UserHowToFM/UHT_FM_7.png)
## Choose Full migration
![8](./Media/UserHowToFM/UHT_FM_7_2.png)
## Fill the destination contract address
![9](./Media/UserHowToFM/UHT_FM_7_3.png)
## This page is a recapitulative of the informations you filled in. Click register.
![10](./Media/UserHowToFM/UHT_FM_8.png)
## Allow the relay to transfer your NFT
![11](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/92730623/145566865-5879c06a-8ef6-4c7a-92cf-ece7cac7334d.png)
![12](./Media/UserHowToFM/UHT_FM_9.png)
## The relay is being approved as an operator of your NFT
![13](./Media/UserHowToFM/UHT_FM_10.png)
## Then the migration process start. First, registering the migration on the origin bridge
![14](./Media/UserHowToFM/UHT_FM_11.png)
## The origin bridge emit a migration hash, you need to sign it to continue
![15](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/92730623/145567373-369c5abd-15af-4823-934d-09e3e26b6715.png)
![16](./Media/UserHowToFM/UHT_FM_12.png)

## The relay is minting your IOU token
![17](./Media/UserHowToFM/UHT_FM_13.png)
## Migration complete ! You now own a IOU NFT token, and can use it as you please. 
![18](./Media/UserHowToFM/UHT_FM_14.png)
## The informations about it are displayed, and you can recover the original token when you want through the same process.
![19](./Media/UserHowToFM/UHT_FM_15.png)
